Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026 · Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Event Ease Balloon Décor NJ (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you visit eventeaseballoondecornj.com (the “Site”) or contact us about balloon décor services. We are based in Rockaway, NJ, United States.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide. When you use our contact form or email us, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, event date, event location, event type, décor preferences, budget range, and any other details you choose to include in messages or form fields.
  • Communications. Records of correspondence when you call, email, or message us, including information needed to respond and follow up.
  • Website usage data. If we use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), we may collect technical information such as approximate location (region/city level), device type, browser type, pages viewed, and referring URLs. Our analytics script only loads if a valid measurement ID is configured; until then, no analytics data is collected through that script.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. Third-party services we use (for example, analytics or embedded maps) may set cookies or use similar technologies according to their own policies.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to contact and inquiry requests about balloon décor services;
  • Schedule consultations, provide pricing, and coordinate installation;
  • Improve the Site and understand how visitors use it (when analytics are enabled);
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights, including detecting spam or abuse.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. How we share information

We may share information with:

  • Form delivery services. Contact form submissions are sent through FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), which processes the form on our behalf and delivers messages to our business email. Their handling of data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Analytics providers. If Google Analytics (or similar) is enabled, usage data may be processed by Google as described in Google’s privacy documentation.
  • Service providers. Vendors that help us operate the Site, email, or related systems, solely as needed to provide those services.
  • Legal requirements. When required by law, court order, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of our business, customers, or others.

The Site is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may process technical data related to hosting and delivery of the Site per GitHub’s policies.

4. Retention

We keep contact and inquiry information for as long as needed to respond to you, provide services, maintain business records, and meet legal or accounting requirements. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the inquiry and applicable law.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we handle. No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information, or to opt out of certain uses (for example, some “sale” or “sharing” activities under U.S. state laws, where applicable).

To exercise rights or ask questions about your information, contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We may need to verify your request before responding.

7. Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. Third-party links and embeds

The Site may link to third-party websites (for example, our sister party-rentals site or map providers) or embed content such as maps. Those services have their own privacy practices; we are not responsible for their policies or content.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy, to the extent permitted by law.
